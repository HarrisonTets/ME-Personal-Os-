# Build Log · Session 11 · 2026-06-20

> **Goal of this session:** Make the app's intelligence **visible**. Feedback was
> that it "works like any other health app" — because the smart, adaptive,
> cross-pillar behaviour was all hidden in the engine. This session surfaces it
> on the Today screen so you can actually *see* ME thinking.

## What we built

Two new things on **Today**, both designed to show what no single-purpose tracker
can:

### 1. The "Your pillars" balance strip
A row of five little rings — one per active pillar — each showing a **0–100
score** of how consistently you've kept up with that area over the last 7 days.

- Weak pillars are instantly obvious (a near-empty ring), strong ones fill up.
- This is the **cross-pillar snapshot** that makes ME more than a health app:
  Finance, Faith, Focus, Health and Learning, balanced in one glance.
- Updates live as you complete habits.

### 2. The "day shape" banner
When your latest reflection said your energy was **low** or **high**, Today now
shows a short banner explaining how it shaped your list:

- Low → *"Gentle day — you logged low energy, so lighter actions come first."*
- High → *"Big day — high energy, so the deeper work is up top."*
- Medium → nothing (no clutter).

This makes the **reflection → suggestion** link — previously invisible — something
you can see and feel. The app reacting to you is now on-screen, not buried.

## The brain behind the strip

- New pure use case `ComputePillarScores`: for each pillar with active habits, it
  measures completed-days ÷ (habits × window) over the last 7 days and returns a
  0–100 score. Pure and unit-tested, like the suggestion engine.

## Tidy-up

We also pulled the "most recent reflected energy" into a single
`dayEnergyProvider`, so the suggestion engine *and* the new banner read from one
source of truth (rather than computing it twice).

## How it behaves

1. Open **Today** → see your five pillar rings up top. Areas you've neglected
   read low; that's your nudge.
2. Complete a few habits → the relevant ring fills in real time.
3. Reflect with **low energy** in the evening → next time Today shows the gentle
   "day shape" banner and a lighter list.

## Why this was the right move

The app was already adaptive — it just never *showed* it. By surfacing pillar
balance and the energy-driven "day shape," ME stops looking like a generic
tracker and starts feeling like a companion that understands your whole life and
responds to how you're doing. Same engine; now you can see it work.
