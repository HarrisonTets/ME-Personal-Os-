## What we built

The Weekly Review (from session 12) showed the **numbers**. Now it has the
**reflection** half too: a weekly check-in that closes the week thoughtfully.

### Weekly reflection
A card at the bottom of the Weekly Review opens a short form asking:

- **How aligned did you feel this week?** (Low / Medium / High)
- **How did your body feel this week?** (Low / Medium / High)
- **One thing to change next week** (optional text)

One reflection per week. If you've already done it, the card shows a summary
("Aligned: High · Body: Medium") and the form pre-fills so you can edit.

### Full slice (the now-familiar recipe)

**Domain:** `WeeklyReflection` entity (reusing the same `Scale` low/med/high type
as daily reflections), `WeeklyReflectionRepository`, and a `SaveWeeklyReflection`
use case.

**Data:** new `WeeklyReflections` table, **migration v8 → v9**, repository impl
that enforces **one reflection per week** (keyed by the week's Monday).

**Presentation:** a weekly reflection form screen, a live
`currentWeekReflectionProvider`, and the card wired into the Weekly Review.

## How it behaves

1. Open **Weekly Review** (📈 on Today).
2. Scroll to the bottom → **Reflect on your week**.
3. Tap the two scales, optionally jot one change, **Save**.
4. Back on the review, the card shows your answers; tap again to edit.

## Reuse worth noting

This was quick because we **reused** a lot: the `Scale` type and its
colours/labels from the daily reflection (session 07), the week-window provider
from Finance (session 03), and the same one-per-period storage pattern. The
clean structure keeps paying off — new features increasingly snap together from
parts we already have.
