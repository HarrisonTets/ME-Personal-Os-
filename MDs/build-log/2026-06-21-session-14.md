## What we built

### A personal greeting on Today
The Today screen now opens with a **time-aware greeting**:
- before noon → "Good morning"
- afternoon → "Good afternoon"
- evening → "Good evening"

…and if you've set your name, it greets you by it: **"Good evening, Harrison."**

### Your life motto
Under the greeting, an optional **life motto** appears in quotes — a phrase to
ground your day (spec mentions an optional "life theme/motto"). Empty by default;
set it and it shows.

### Editing your profile
Settings now has a **Profile** section at the top with two fields — **your name**
and **life motto** — and a Save button. Both are stored in the settings table and
appear instantly on Today.

### The app's real name
The Android app label changed from `personal_os` to **ME**, so under the icon on
your phone it now reads **ME** instead of the project's code name.

## How it behaves

1. Open **Settings** (⚙️ on Today) → **Profile** → type your name and a motto →
   **Save profile**.
2. Go back to Today → it greets you by name with your motto beneath.
3. After reinstalling, the home-screen icon is labelled **ME**.

## Note on the app icon

The app **name** is now ME, but the **icon** is still Flutter's default. A real
icon needs an image file; when you have a logo, the standard way to apply it is
the `flutter_launcher_icons` package (a one-time setup). Flagged as a small
ship-time task.
