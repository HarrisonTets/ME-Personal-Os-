### 2. The Spiritual / Bible pillar (full slice)

**The brain (domain):**
- New entities: `BiblePlan`, `ReadingAssignment`, `JournalEntry`.
- New storage promise: `SpiritualRepository`.
- New rule (`CompleteQuietTime`): finishing a reading does two things at once тАФ
  marks the day done **and** saves your reflection to the journal (if you wrote
  one).

**The memory (data):**
- Three new database tables: `BiblePlans`, `ReadingAssignments`, `JournalEntries`.
- A **migration** so your *existing* database (with your habits) safely upgrades
  to include these new tables without losing anything. (DB version 1 тЖТ 2.)
- A seeded **"Gospel of John"** plan: 21 days, one chapter each.
- `SpiritualRepositoryImpl` to read/write it all.

**The face (presentation):**
- **Bible home screen**: a colorful plan card with a progress bar, today's
  reading, and your reflections timeline.
- **Quiet Time screen**: shows the passage to read, a reflection prompt, a text
  box, and a "Mark reading complete" button.
- **Journal**: every reflection you save appears on the Bible home, newest first.

## How it behaves

1. Open the **Bible** tab тЖТ see "Gospel of John, Day 1 ┬╖ John 1".
2. Tap **Begin Quiet Time** тЖТ a screen slides in with the passage + a reflection
   box.
3. Write a thought (optional) and tap **Mark reading complete**.
4. You bounce back to the Bible home: progress moves to "1 of 21," tomorrow's
   reading becomes "John 2," and your reflection shows under **Reflections**.
5. Finish all 21 тЖТ a celebration card appears.

## A design note: where's the actual Bible text?

Right now we show the **reference** (e.g. "John 3") and ask you to read it in your
own Bible. We deliberately did **not** bundle the full Bible text yet, because
Bible translations have **copyright/licensing** rules we need to choose
carefully (which translation? bundled offline, or fetched from an API?). That's a
decision for a future session тАФ noted so it isn't forgotten.
