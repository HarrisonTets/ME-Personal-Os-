# Build Log · Session 12 · 2026-06-21

> **Goal of this session:** Two upgrades that lean into what makes ME different —
> make the pillar rings **interactive**, and add a **Weekly Review** that ties
> every pillar together in one place.

## What we built

### 1. Tappable pillar rings
The pillar balance rings on Today are now **buttons**. Tap one and the app jumps
straight to that pillar's tab (Finance ring → Finance tab, etc.). It turns the
"here's where you stand" strip into a navigation shortcut.

- To make this work, the bottom tab bar now reads its selected tab from a shared
  `homeTabProvider`, so any screen can switch tabs. (Previously the tab index was
  locked inside the nav bar.)
- The Learning ring has no tab yet, so it simply does nothing when tapped.

### 2. Weekly Review
A new screen (the **📈 icon** top-right on Today) that summarises your whole week
across every pillar in one view:

- **The pillar balance strip** (reused from Today) at the top.
- **Four key numbers**: actions completed, minutes focused, money spent, Bible
  readings done — pulled from four different pillars.
- **"Looking ahead"** insight: your strongest pillar this week, and a gentle
  nudge toward the weakest one for next week.

This is the "step back and see the whole picture" moment that a single-purpose
health/finance/habit app structurally can't give you — it only knows its one
thing. ME knows all five.

## The interesting bit: composing across pillars

The Weekly Review pulls from **five different repositories at once** (habits,
focus, finance, spiritual, plus the pillar-score engine). We did this in one
`weeklyReviewProvider` that reads each source once and combines them into a
single `WeeklySummary`.

This was only painless because of the clean architecture: each pillar already
exposes its data through a tidy repository, so "gather everything for the week"
is just calling each one and adding up the results — no tangled code.

## How it behaves

1. On **Today**, tap a pillar ring → you land on that pillar's tab.
2. Tap the **📈** icon (top-right) → the Weekly Review opens.
3. See your balance, the week's numbers, and a look-ahead nudge.
